Our Story

Passion on a Plate

Established to bring the warmth of European dining to your city, The Velvet Table is a sanctuary for food lovers. We believe in the magic of slow cooking, rich wines, and conversations that last all night.

Our burgundy-themed interiors reflect our devotion to red wines and robust flavors, while our service ensures you feel like royalty.
